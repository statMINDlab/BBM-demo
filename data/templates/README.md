## Alternative templates 
Commonly used functional parcellations of the human brain are available but have not been provided here since they require a User Agreement or are otherwise not publicly available. Below, we present a list of additional parcellations whose products are included in this repository.

### HCP
HCP group ICA functional maps can be obtained from the HCP S1200 release, available within [ConnectomeDB](https://balsa.wustl.edu) tool after signing a Data User Agreement.

### Conte69 
Is also available at [this location](https://balsa.wustl.edu/file/J2Vn) within the connectomeDB.
